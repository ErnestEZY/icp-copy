### Technical Role Resume Guidelines
1. Focus on technical stacks and frameworks.
2. Highlight specific projects and their impact.
3. Include links to GitHub, Portfolio, or relevant technical blogs.
4. List certifications from reputable providers (AWS, Azure, Google, etc.).
5. Ensure technical terms are used correctly and contextually.
6. Emphasize problem-solving skills and technical leadership.
7. Mention experience with Agile/Scrum and version control.
8. Show evidence of continuous learning.
9. Differentiate between proficiency levels for different technologies.
10. Highlight contributions to open-source projects.
