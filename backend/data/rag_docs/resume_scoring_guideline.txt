### Resume Scoring Guideline
1. Formatting and Layout (10 points)
2. Grammar and Spelling (10 points)
3. Professional Summary (10 points)
4. Work Experience and Achievements (30 points)
5. Skills and Competencies (20 points)
6. Education and Certifications (10 points)
7. Overall Impression and Tailoring (10 points)
Total Score: 100 points
