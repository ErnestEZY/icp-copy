### Creative Role Resume Guidelines
1. Prioritize a visual portfolio or link to creative work.
2. Use a visually appealing and modern resume layout.
3. Highlight creative software proficiency (Adobe Suite, Figma, etc.).
4. Emphasize storytelling, brand development, and conceptual thinking.
5. Mention awards, exhibitions, or notable creative projects.
6. Show evidence of collaboration with cross-functional teams.
7. Highlight experience with user-centered design or creative direction.
8. Use creative but professional language to describe work.
9. Include testimonials or references from creative peers.
10. Emphasize adaptability and ability to work on diverse creative briefs.
