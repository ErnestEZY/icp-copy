### Business Role Resume Guidelines
1. Focus on leadership, management, and strategic thinking.
2. Highlight measurable achievements (revenue growth, cost reduction, efficiency improvements).
3. Emphasize soft skills like communication, negotiation, and teamwork.
4. Use professional business language and avoid overly technical jargon.
5. Highlight experience with market analysis, financial modeling, or business development.
6. Mention relevant MBA or business-related certifications.
7. Show evidence of stakeholder management and networking.
8. Emphasize ability to work in fast-paced, high-pressure environments.
9. Highlight international experience or multilingual capabilities.
10. Use a clean, professional, and standard resume format.
