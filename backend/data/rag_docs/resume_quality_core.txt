### Resume Quality Core
1. Consistency in formatting, fonts, and layout.
2. Error-free grammar, spelling, and punctuation.
3. Clear and concise language with active verbs.
4. Logical flow and organization of information.
5. Relevant and up-to-date contact information.
6. Effective use of white space and bullet points.
7. Targeted and tailored content for specific roles.
8. Strong and impactful professional summary or objective.
9. Professional and appropriate tone throughout.
10. Easy to read and scan for recruiters and ATS.
