### Academic Role Resume Guidelines
1. Focus on research, publications, and teaching experience.
2. Highlight academic degrees, honors, and awards.
3. Include a comprehensive list of research projects and grants.
4. Emphasize pedagogical skills and curriculum development.
5. Mention conference presentations and academic service.
6. Show evidence of collaboration with other researchers and institutions.
7. Highlight experience with academic advising or mentoring.
8. Use formal academic language and standard citation styles.
9. Include references from academic supervisors or peers.
10. Emphasize commitment to academic integrity and continuous research.
