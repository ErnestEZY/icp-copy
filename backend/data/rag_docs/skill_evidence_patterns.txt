### Skill Evidence Patterns
1. Using quantified achievements (e.g., "Increased sales by 20%").
2. Describing specific projects and their outcomes.
3. Mentioning relevant tools, software, or technologies.
4. Highlighting leadership or teamwork experience.
5. Showing evidence of problem-solving and critical thinking.
6. Using industry-specific terminology correctly.
7. Mentioning certifications, training, or professional development.
8. Describing responsibilities and duties in detail.
9. Showing progression and growth in previous roles.
10. Including testimonials or endorsements where appropriate.
